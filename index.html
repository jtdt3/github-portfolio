0-1 Knapsack problem

The 0-1 knapsack problem is an optimization problem, where you have a set of items with a weight and benefit. You also have the total weight that your knapsack can hold. The goal is to figure out which items to include in the knapsack, that maximizes the total value, without going over the weight limit. With "0-1" meaning we can either include the item or not include it.
